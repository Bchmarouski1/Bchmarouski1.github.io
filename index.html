About Me

Hola! I'M Bernice Chmarouski

I am a freelance designer and founder of Urs Truly Design from Salley South Carolina, a mom of five, yes, you read correctly, five fantastic children, and three spoiled dogs. My creations have appeared in F.I.R.S.T. Institute and Sings2Go. In addition, my work can be viewed on Behance, Instagram, and Facebook.

Inspired by color and motivated by out-of-the-box creativity, I have competed at the A.C.S.P. Competitions landing first place trophy in special effects makeup and second place in catwalk couture makeup. These events have taught me teamwork, attention to detail, leadership skills, and comprehensive knowledge of color theory. Some of my hobbies are drawing whimsical illustrations, reading, makeup, and photography.

I’ve helped artists, boutiques, beauty salons, small companies, and insanely hard workers create their brands and their online cultures, which have transcended across their industries, and helped build their businesses. 

I put all my Heart in.

I work closely with my clients, forming trust-built relationships that instigate and inspire a shared long-term vision. This means learning deeply about their mission, and how best I can support their brand influence, all while setting the best stage possible.

I am passionate about creativity, my work is illustrative, fun, and I live life with color. If you are ready to Create your Brand, Elevate and Grow your business, Urs Truly Design would be an excellent fit for your design needs and business growth.
